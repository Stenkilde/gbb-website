2016matches

All our 2016 GBB Matches have been created for all the players, the golfers and the members at selected and leading Nordic Golf Clubs. Furthermore, by chosing these venues, we also have incorporated a very strong ambition to serve and care for our large group of high profile Sponsors, Partners and elite Event Suppliers.

We are very proud and quite honoured to have landed proper and exclusive interest, support and dedication from these fine golf clubs and we cannot wait to get out there and host excellent GBB Matches with all our luxury prizes, goodies, happenings and surprises.

The 2016Matches calendar is still looking for an additional 3-4 venues, preferably in the Autumn/September/October 2016  -  so, should you by any chance bear the title as Golf Club Manager or Golf Club Director at a leading Danish, Norwegian or Swedish Golf Club and in desperate need of luxury golf event for your members  -  then please contact us asap.

We have formed this group to excite and thrill prominent golf club members  -  in all of Nordic Area.
